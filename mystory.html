August 2015 - Microsoft DigiGirlz High Tech Camp & AP Computer Science

I was first exposed to the world of computer science in the summer of my sophomore year in high school when I participated in the Microsoft DigGirlz High Tech Camp.

Through the motivational tech talks given from Microsoft employees & speakers, I was inspired to take the AP Computer Science class at my high school. Computer science was no easy subject for me and coming in to the class as a beginner, I was intimidated by the dominating group of male students. Taking on the challenge of going into a field where I had no previous experience with, I learned to practice coding on my own & go in during tutorial hours at school to reach out for help.

Furthermore, I learned to be inattentive to the discouragement I received along the way & use criticism as fuel to do even better. Through the long nights of practice, I adapted to coding concepts and gradually gained more self confidence in my own work.

Thankfully to the wise words from the Microsoft Digi Girlz Camp always in the back of my mind, I not only accomplished the goals I set out for myself, but also developed new skills & the hobby of coding. Microsoft DigiGirlz Camp was definitely a phenomenal experience to help me discover the foundational steps to pursuing a career in tech.
