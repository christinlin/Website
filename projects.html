Medzii Healthcare Treatment iOS App | June 18-September 13 , 2018

In the summer of 2018, I interned at Edifecs, a healthcare company in Bellevue working on the start-up app, Medzii.

The Medzii App is a person treatment planning app aimed to help individuals track their own health including reminders of their daily supplements. During my summer internship working on the Medzii project, I worked on the new machine learning function to calculate drug-to-drug interactions from FAERS (FDA Adverse Event Reporting System) external data set. In the last few weeks, I also worked on AWS Lambda Function to custom the Medzii APIs domain names & improve security.

Techologies & Languages: Back End - Python, Data Mining, SQLite, Pandas Library, AWS

Local Expert Media Vault Functionality | June 26-August 16, 2017

As an intern at Expedia Group, I was placed on the Local Expert Partner Central Team (LXPC) and worked on the Media Vault Functionality of the Local Expert site.

The Expedia Local Expert Team is in charge of the "places to go" when customers select their desired travel destination. It guides customers to the main attractions in their location through organizing activities on the Local Expert site. I worked on both the front end and back end of developing the Media Vault Functionality. This tool helped Local Expert employees to improve efficiency of approving and rejecting photos (based off asset, type, and activity IDs) from suppliers such as Disney and Universal Studios who want their photos for advertisng purposes on Expedia activities.

Techologies & Languages: Front End - HTML/CSS, Back End - Javascript, Ember.js Framework


Brag Log | August 13, 2017

I competed in the Tune Inc. WE HACK 2017! all-women hackathon and took home the first place trophy for my team's project of Brag Log Mobile SMS Messaging System.

The theme of the hackathon was "Women Empowerment," therefore my team and I decided to create an SMS messaging system that records your daily logs of the little and big things individuals like us achieve every single day. Due to the commonality of imposter syndrome in the workplace & school, we target Brag Log helps to help individuals switch their mindsets and be reminded of the great things they do and self-care with weekly reports texted to their phones.

Technologies & Languages: Front End - HTML/CSS, Back End - Python, Twilio

inTune | February 19, 2017

During Code Day Eastside at T-Mobile HQ, my team and I created a website targetted towards people with disabilities and inability to express what they feel. We won the "Special Award" for our project's creativity and design.

The tool we developed allowed users to be able to type in their feelings and current mood into the textbox. We used the Microsoft Cognitive Services API to detect the user's mood from a scale of 0.0 (super sad)-1.0 (super happy). From this data, we were able to decipher what song (in separate array lists based off mood scale) to play in order to soothe their feelings and engage in mindfulness practice.

Technologies & Lanugages: Front End - HTML/CSS, Back End - Microsoft Cognitive Services API & Java
