SOFTWARE ENGINEER + DESIGNER

Hello! My name is Christin and I am an undergraduate sophomore attending the University of Washington Seattle in the Washington State Academic Redshirt (STARS) Engineering Program intending to major in Computer Science & Engineering.

As a college student, my goal is to explore various technologies and programming languages in order to advance my knowledge in computing & open career and internship opportunities to accelerate my tech career.

I became motivated and passionate to study engineering after attending Microsoft DigiGirlz High Tech Camp in the summer of my sophomore year in high school. In addition, I challenged myself to enroll in the AP Computer Science class offered at my high school. Ever since, I've participated and competed in a diverse range of hackathons throughout the Seattle-Bellevue area to bring my own ideas out to the table.

Additionally, in the summer of my senior year in high school, I interned at Expedia Inc. HQ in Bellevue as a Software Developer Apprentice on the Local Expert Partner Central (LXPC) team working on the Media Vault Functionality project. Last summer I interned at Edifecs, a leading healthcare technology company in Bellevue, to gain insight on the concept of Machine Learning & its technologies on the app, Medzii. Furthermore, I am currently working at T-Mobile HQ in Bellevue participating in the TechX Internship Program on the T-Mobile Tuesdays Android Development Team.
