ABOUT ME

Life as an Engineer

Hello! My name is Christin and I am a college sophomore attending the University of Washington Seattle in the Washington State Academic Redshirt (STARS) Engineering Program intending to major in Computer Science & Engineering.

As a college student, my goal is to explore various technologies and programming languages in order to advance my knowledge in computing & open career and internship opportunities to accelerate my tech career. Furthermore, I am starting my own personal projects this summer & will be posting about my progress through my blog/projects.

I became motivated and passionate to study engineering after attending Microsoft DigiGirlz High Tech Camp, I challenged myself to enroll in the AP Computer Science class offered at my high school. Ever since, I've participated and competed in a diverse range of hackathons throughout the Seattle-Bellevue area to bring my own ideas out to the table.

Additionally, last summer I interned at Expedia Inc. HQ in Bellevue as a Software Developer Apprentice on the Local Expert Partner Central (LXPC) team working on the Media Vault Functionality project. This summer I will be interning at Edifecs, a leading healthcare technology company in Bellevue to gain insight on the concept of Machine Learning. In the fall, I will be interning at T-Mobile HQ as a Software Developer on the Digital Development & Innovations Team.

Come check out my blog! I will be featuring my adventures & challenges in school with the STARS Program in my second year of college. Additionally, I will be talking about my internship experiences and just overall a weekly update about what I am doing & what I am up to! :)

Take a look at my previous projects from the hackathons I've competed in and from programs I have participated in! I will also be adding in a few descriptions and updates on my own personal projects I am working on! ^-^ I hope you enjoy and provide me some feedback, thank you so much!

Welcome to the section encompassing my skills & experience along with where I am heading in terms of my tech career! I hope this helps to shed some light and give a better insight on my professional expertise along with my pursuit in a career in engineering.

Thank you so much for reading through my website, I really appreciate your interest in my work. Please feel free to reach out to me, I'd love to hear from you & receive some feedback! As always, onward & upwards!
