Life as an Engineer

Hello! My name is Christin and I am a undergraduate freshman attending the University of Washington Seattle in the Washington State Academic (STARS) Engineering Program intending to major in Computer Science & Engineering.

As a college freshman, my goal is to be exposed to technologies and master coding languages in order to create my own projects. I became interested in the tech field after hearing from speakers at Microsoft speakers at the DigiGirlz High Tech Camp in the summer of my sophomore year in high school. That school year, I challenged myself to take the AP Computer Science class offered at my high school and later participated in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. Ever since then, I've participated and competed in hackathons around the Seattle-Bellevue area to bring my own ideas out to the table.

Additionally, I had the opportunity to intern at Expedia Inc. HQ in Bellevue last summer as a Software Developer Apprentice on the Local Expert Partner Central team working on the Media Vault Functionality of the website. This summer I will be working at Edifecs, a leading healthcare technology company with the mission to improve healthcare outcomes, reduce costs and accelerate innovation. This coming fall, I will be interning at T-Mobile HQ in Bellevue as a Software Engineer on the Digital Development & Innovation Team helping to improve T-Mobile's innovation across digital development through CI/CD/CT best practices. using cloud based platforms such as Amazaon WebServices and Azure.
